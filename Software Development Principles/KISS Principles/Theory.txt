KISS (Keep it simple, stupid) PRINCIPLE:
----------------
1.The KISS principle states that systems work best if they are kept simple rather than made complex.
2. Don't over-engineer. Favor simple, readable, and maintainable solutions.

WHY KISS?
--------
1.Easier to read, debug, and maintain.
2.Reduces the risk of bugs and technical debt.
3.Faster onboarding for new developers.
