DRY PRINCIPLES
--------------
1.It stands for do not repeat your self.
2. It is an software development principles which reduces code repeatation.

WHY FOLLOW DRY?
--------------
1. Maintainablity:Fixes and updates are easiear and localized as make changes at single place is enough.
2. Readability: Easier for you and others to understand.
3. Testability: Resuable components/methods are easier to test in isolation.
