YAGNI PRINCIPLE:
---------------
1.It stands for You Aren’t Gonna Need It.
2.YAGNI means don’t add functionality until it’s actually needed.
3.Key Idea: "Build only what is required right now."

WHY YAGNI?
---------
1. Saves time and effort.
2. Keeps the codebase clean and focused. not make the code base messy.
3. Reduces the risk of bugs in unused code.
4. Makes testing and maintenance easier.
